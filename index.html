01 The Core Problem

How do you automatically tell if a trending topic is a real news event — or just internet noise?
✅ Real Event
  • Sustained media coverage over days
  • Growing article velocity
  • Multiple independent sources
  • Appears in GDELT database
❌ Internet Noise
  • Peaks and dies within hours
  • Stays on a single platform
  • No news source coverage
  • Not in GDELT database

Our solution: track Semantic Velocity — how fast a topic grows — and verify against real news

05 Advanced ML: LDA

Every document is a MIX of topics. Every topic is a distribution over words.

Step 1 — Topic Mix
Sample topic distribution θ from Dirichlet(α)
Ex: 60% Politics, 30% Health, 10% Tech
Step 2 — Pick Topic Per Word
For each word, sample topic z from Multinomial(θ)
Ex: word 1 → Politics, word 2 → Health
Step 3 — Pick Word
Sample word from topic's vocabulary β
Ex: Politics → "president", Health → "covid"
LDA's limitation: Static corpus assumption — treats 2012 and 2022 articles identically. This directly motivates our 3 novel features.

06 Novel Feature Engineering

01Temporal Weight w(d) = log(1 + Δt) / max(log(1 + Δt))

Recent articles weighted higher. Mean = 0.88

02Category Velocity V(c,t) = [N(c,Wt) - N(c,Wt-1)] / N(c,Wt-1)

Growth rate at time of publication

03Text Richness R(d) = |Unique Words| / (|Total Words| + 1)

Semantic density. Mean = 0.86

10 Failure Analysis

The Failure: COVID & Court topic peaks in 2014 — five years before COVID-19 existed.

2014 — ACA Legal Challenges

Top words: state, health, court, people

Context: Affordable Care Act court battles over health mandates.

2020 — COVID-19 Pandemic

Top words: state, health, court, people

Context: COVID pandemic, state health lockdowns, court challenges.

LDA's bag-of-words assumption strips context entirely. Both eras share identical vocabulary → cosine similarity ≈ 1.0 → same topic. Phase 2 fix: SBERT encodes full sentence meaning, separating these two contexts in embedding space.

This failure is a strength — it precisely identifies LDA's limitation and scientifically motivates Phase 2.
